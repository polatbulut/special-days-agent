Event-Driven Demand Forecasting

A multi-agent architecture that turns live event data into airline demand forecasts. Regional agents ingest ticketing, fixture, trade-fair and holiday feeds, prediction agents estimate impact and attendance, and the results feed the Expected Load Factor and Expected Revenue projects.
